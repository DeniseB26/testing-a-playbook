1
Log In & Check Schedule
Log into Netpark
2
Pull & Print Reservations by Zone
Go to Reservations, select today's date, then filter by zone
Click "Name" at the top of the list to alphabetize by last name
Scan for any balance due / refund due
Print each zone in this order (fewest reservations print first, stack ends up in correct order):
1st — 7707 Tesla Chargers
2nd — 7707 Discounted Outdoor Parking - No Shuttle
3rd — 7707 Covered - Awnings
Staple all pages together into one packet
ℹ️ Due: $0.00 = no balance owed (normal). Any other amount needs follow-up.
3
Text Customers
📱 Google Voice is limited to 20 texts/day. If total reservations exceed 20, send the extras the night before using the "for tomorrow" version of the text.
Text each customer individually (not as a group), working through each zone list
Standard Text — Morning Of (all zones)
Please read and respond: your [TESLA/EV — include only for Tesla zone] cruise parking reservation is at Port Parking's Discount Outdoor lot at 7707 Harborside Dr., Galveston, TX 77554.

Due to construction on I-45 and near the cruise terminals, please allow extra travel time.

Check-in starts at 9 AM. We generally close at 2 PM, but if you're sailing on the MSC Seascape we stay open an extra hour — just be at the lot no later than 2 PM. Everyone else, please arrive by 1 PM.

Note: There's no shuttle at this lot, and it's several miles from the terminals so you need to arrange for a taxi, Uber, Lyft, or other ride.

Please respond with an ETA and if you have questions, I'll try to help. Safe travels!
Night Before Text (when over 20 reservations)
Same as above, but change "is at" to "is for tomorrow at" and change the last line to:
"Please respond in the morning (or tonight if you prefer) with an ETA..."
⚠️ If a customer says they need to cancel:
Cancellation Response
Thank you for letting us know. Please send your cancellation request to [manager email]. Safe travels!
In Netpark: add " - CANCEL" after their last name (e.g., Bell → Bell - CANCEL)
In the Notes field: add "Customer notified to send email to manager. - [your initials]"
Save the reservation
Tracking Sent Texts on Printed List:
Txt 8:15 Write time text was sent under customer name
TXT Add "TXT" to last name in Netpark if texted night before
4
Handle Responses & ETAs
When a customer responds with an ETA, write "ETA [time]" on the printed list after their text time (e.g., Txt 8:15    ETA 9:30)
Reply with the appropriate arrival message:
Standard Arrival Reply (Regular & Covered)
Thank you. When you arrive, please take any available spot near the stop sign when you come into the lot. It's temporary. Come in the office and I'll check you in and show you where to park.
Tesla Arrival Reply
Thank you. When you arrive, you'll see the TESLA chargers on the right side of the lot as you enter. Please pull into one of those spots and come into the office to check in.
5
Follow-Up Calls — Start at 12:00 PM
⏰ At 12:00 PM, call any customer who has not responded to their text.
Check your printed list for customers without an ETA noted
Call each non-responding customer
Voicemail Script (substitute your name)
"Hi, this is Denise with Port Parking. You have a reservation to park with us during your cruise which starts today. I sent you a text message with some important information and asking for an ETA but I haven't heard from you so I thought I'd give you a call. Please read and respond to the text but if it's easier to call, please call us at 855-435-7678. Thank you."
After leaving a voicemail, note it on the printed list (e.g., Txt 8:15    VM 12:30)
If a customer calls back, note it in the last name field in Netpark. If no note exists and you haven't heard back, follow up again.
